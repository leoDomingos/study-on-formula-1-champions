Explanation of the files in /data:
offes = one file for every season

all-seasons-constructorstandings: The constructor standings after the season end. (offes)
all-seasons-driverstandings: The drivers standings after the season. (offes)
all-seasons-first-races: Data about the first race of every season. (offes)
all-seasons-results: Overview of every season result. (offes)
2008-season: Details about only the 2008 season.
constructors-1: Names of every team who has ever won a WCC.
seasons: Contains, among other things, the number of seasons the API has.
